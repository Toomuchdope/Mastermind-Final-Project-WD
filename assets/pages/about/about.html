ABOUT US

Our Story

It was during the COVID pandemic in 2020 that But First, Coffee (BFC) was founded as a digital coffee shop. While everyone stayed at home, BFC offered a way for its customers to have access to good coffee without breaking the bank.

Since then, BFC has been featured in numerous vlogs, blogs, and has received rave reviews from well-known personalities, influencers, and regular customers. BFC prides itself on being a brand that aims to help the community especially the local farmers who supply their coffee beans.

Values

We support local farmers by using local coffee beans, and through our collective technical expertise, create a high quality product that can compete with the biggest names while remaining affordable for every Filipino. Our best-tasting coffee, together with free delivery service, make us accessible for anyone looking for a good cup of coffee, anytime, anywhere.

Mission

Our mission is to provide affordable but high quality coffee for everyone.

Vision

We aim to expand to every major city, business district, and residential area in Metro Manila by the end of 2024, prime tourist spots of the country such as Baguio, La Union, Coron, Siargao, El Nido by 2025, and hopefully, help the flag of Filipino coffee beans fly high outside the country by 2030.

Products

Our products revolve around coffee and milk-based drinks for non-coffee lovers. Our best sellers are Vietnamese and Spanish Latte. Barako Beans are used for Drip-Based and Arabica Beans for Espresso-Based. We also have pastries, sandwiches, and snacks that can be perfectly paired with any of our drinks.
